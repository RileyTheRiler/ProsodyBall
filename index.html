⚽ Prosody Ball
Control a bouncing ball with your voice. Pitch bounces, tempo changes speed, vowels leave trails.
① Bounciness
Vary your pitch up and down. Bigger pitch swings = higher bounces.
② Tempo Variance
Change your speaking speed. Fast→slow→fast makes the ball accelerate and decelerate.
③ Vowel Elongation
Stretch your vowels ("hellooo"). Sustained sounds create a glowing trail.
④ Articulation
Crisp consonants create sparkle effects. Pronounce clearly!
⑤ Syllable Separation
Distinct syllable breaks trigger individual bounce events. E-NUN-CI-ATE!
🐉 Voice Creature
Your voice becomes a living being. Resonance shapes its body, prosody transforms it.
Pitch → Float
Higher pitch lifts the creature. It floats when your voice rises.
Resonance → Form
Resonance shapes its body — dark/compact to bright/tall. It IS your vocal tract.
Vowels → Tendrils
Sustained sounds grow flowing tendrils. The longer you hold, the more they extend.
Articulation → Spark
Sharp consonants make the surface crackle with light. Be crisp!
Prosody → Transform
Sustained expressiveness unlocks wings, glow, and luminous patterns.
🌱 Voice Garden
Grow a living ecosystem with your voice. Every session adds plants that accumulate.
Pitch → Species
Low pitch grows mushrooms, mid grows flowers, high grows tall trees. Vary to diversify!
Resonance → Color
Bright resonance makes vivid, warm plants. Dark resonance grows cool, blue-toned flora.
Vowels → Sunlight
Sustained sounds are photosynthesis — they accelerate growth. Hold vowels to watch plants surge.
Articulation → Pollen
Crisp consonants scatter pollen and firefly particles through the garden.
Syllables → Seeds
Each distinct syllable plants a new seed. More syllables = denser garden.
🎨 Voice Canvas
Paint generative art with your voice. Each session creates a unique piece you can save.
Pitch → Y Position
Your pitch paints vertically — low voice at the bottom, high voice sweeps to the top.
Resonance → Color
Dark resonance paints cool blues, bright resonance paints warm golds and pinks.
Vowels → Curves
Sustained sounds create smooth flowing brushstrokes. Hold vowels for long, graceful lines.
Articulation → Texture
Sharp consonants splatter paint droplets and add texture to the canvas.
Energy → Width
Louder, more energetic speech creates wider, bolder strokes. Whisper for fine detail.

✨ Pitch Pilot
Navigate the resonance cavern with your voice, steering a luminous vocal spark through crystal barriers.
Pitch → Altitude
The vocal spark maps to your absolute pitch, so stable notes hold altitude precisely.
Quick Calibration
At run start, glide from low to high so the cavern adapts to your personal vocal range.
Silence = Gravity

Discord Barriers

Phase Progression

🏍️ Resonance Road
Steer a sonic speeder with vocal timbre while reading a teleprompter. Stay on-tone or skid into the hazard lane.
Target Resonance
Pick Bright or Dark posture. The road color palette flips to match your training goal.
Voice = Engine
Speaking drives forward speed. Confidence increases pace and scoring potential.
Timbre = Steering
Maintain target tone color to stay centered. Drift in timbre and the speeder veers off-road.
Hazard Feedback
Off-road creates messy hazard trails, speed drag, and multiplier loss.
Session Replay
Your trail records control through the passage, making weak words easy to identify.
🎈 Spectral Ascent
Vocal weight directly drives altitude. A live spectral tilt gauge exposes the raw algorithm output.
Spectral Weight → Altitude
Light/breathy phonation lifts the balloon, heavy/buzzy phonation sinks it with direct 1-to-1 mapping.
Live Tilt Gauge
A vertical gauge shows MAX LIGHT, NEUTRAL, and MAX HEAVY with a tethered marker linked to the balloon.
Single Vowel Constraint
Hold one neutral vowel ("Ah"/"Uh") and vary only vocal weight for cleaner spectral tilt diagnostics.
Spectral Gates
Fly through high, low, neutral, and staircase gate patterns to stress-test calibration, drift, and response speed.
Post-Flight Diagnostics
Run reports Spectral Latency, Stability, and Dynamic Range so users can inspect algorithm behavior directly.
🔮 Prism Reader
Read text aloud and watch each syllable crystallize with color, glow, and texture from your voice.
Pitch → Hue
Low pitch crystallizes blue. High pitch crystallizes pink. Each syllable gets a permanent color from your voice.
Resonance → Glow
Forward, bright resonance creates luminous text glow. Dark resonance stays flat and matte.
Weight → Edge
Heavy vocal weight gives sharp, crisp text edges. Breathy voice creates soft, fuzzy syllable outlines.
Onset Stepping
Each syllable onset in your speech advances the reader. Speak naturally to progress through the text.
Manual Mode
Press spacebar or tap to manually advance syllables. Use this if onset detection misaligns.
⛰️ Vowel Valley
Navigate a 2D space using your vocal formants. Train precise vowel shaping by visiting target zones.
F2 → Horizontal (Left/Right)
Front vs Back vowels. Making an "EE" sound moves you right, while "OO" moves you left.
F1 → Vertical (Up/Down)
High vs Low vowels. Lowering your jaw ("AH") moves you down; raising it ("EE"/"OO") moves you up.
Target Zones
Navigate to the "EE", "AH", and "OO" zones to score. Maintain the vowel to charge the zone.
Vocal Tract Feedback
The visual position reflects your actual vocal tract configuration in real-time.
Flow Scoring
Smoothly transitioning between target vowels earns "Flow" bonuses.
