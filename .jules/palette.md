## 2026-03-30 - [Inaccessible Tab Widgets]
**Learning:** Discovered that the `.help-tabs` component lacked semantic ARIA roles (`tablist`, `tab`, `tabpanel`) and relationship attributes (`aria-controls`, `aria-labelledby`, `aria-selected`), making it difficult for screen reader users to understand the structure and active state of the tabs.
**Action:** Always implement full ARIA tab patterns for custom tab widgets. Ensure the container has `role="tablist"`, tabs have `role="tab"` with `aria-controls` and `aria-selected` (dynamically updated), and panels have `role="tabpanel"` with `aria-labelledby`.
